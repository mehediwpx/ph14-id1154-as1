Frequently Asked Questions

Are there group discounts available?

Yes, we offer custom team packages. The standard Team tier covers up to 10 attendees, but if you have a larger group, please reach out to our support team for a tailored quote.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Absolutely. The conference center is fully ADA compliant, with elevator access to all floors, designated seating in keynotes, and accessible restrooms throughout the venue.

Can I switch my workshop choices later?

Yes, you can modify your workshop schedule via the attendee portal up until one week before the conference begins, subject to availability.
